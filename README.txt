3. Installation Guide
----------------------------------------------------------
1. Unzip the package.
2. Open "index.html" in your browser to view the template.
3. To upload on your server:
   - Copy all files to your hosting directory.
   - Make sure the "assets" folder structure remains intact.

----------------------------------------------------------
4. Dark/Light Mode Usage
----------------------------------------------------------
- By default, Light Mode is active.
- Click the toggle button (Moon 🌙 or Sun ☀️ icon at top-right) to switch modes.
